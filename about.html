Who Are We?

The Getback Machine was created by Lucas Sawtelle and Dan Glover with a shared passion for preserving history through collectibles.

Lucas is an 18-year-old entrepreneur and college student building the next generation of collectible sales while bringing fresh ideas, technology, and ambition to the business.

Dan is a former truck driver turned collector whose years on the road gave him a unique eye for rare finds and the stories behind them.

Through his booth at Highway Pickers, Dan shares his discoveries with fellow collectors and continues building a community around forgotten treasures.

Together, Lucas and Dan combine experience and new ideas to give old collectibles a new life.
